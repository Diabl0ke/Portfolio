TheWeather app

Shall I take an umbrella?

Here is a small project of mine, just a simple weather app so that you know if you need to take an umbrella or not. It uses some javascrpt, and an API, it's that simple !

As you can see, you have the current weather, how it is going to look like the rest of the day temperature wise, and weather forecast on a week.

Try it out using this link : https://diabl0ke.github.io/app_meteo/
